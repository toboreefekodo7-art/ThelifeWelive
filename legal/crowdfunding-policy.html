Campaign Submission

Campaigns may be submitted by individuals, families, students, organizations, or nominators. TLWL may request proof of need, identity, supporting documents, and recipient consent.

Verification Before Contributions

Campaigns requesting financial or community support should be reviewed before contributions open. TLWL may require identity checks, proof of need, recipient consent, or additional documentation.

Campaign Use of Funds

Funds should be used according to the stated purpose on the campaign page. If circumstances change, TLWL may pause, revise, refund, or redirect funds according to the disclosed campaign terms.

Updates

TLWL should provide reasonable updates when possible to show campaign progress and impact.

Reports and Fraud Prevention

TLWL may review reports from visitors and may pause, clear, remove, or mark a campaign for refund review if information appears false, misleading, fraudulent, harmful, or otherwise concerning.
