Crowdfunding Policy

TLWL's crowdfunding model is built around storytelling, transparency, trust, and community support. TLWL is a for-profit social impact and crowdfunding platform.

Campaign Submission

Campaigns may be submitted by individuals, families, students, organizations, or nominators. Required campaign information may include organizer name, beneficiary name, relationship to beneficiary, fund purpose, fund use, delivery plan, written story, video, consent, and acknowledgement of TLWL's for-profit platform disclaimer.

Public Campaigns and Contributions

A campaign may become public and accept contributions when required information is complete and basic platform checks pass. TLWL does not intend to manually approve every contribution before a supporter can contribute.

Payout Verification

Campaign funds should not be released to a recipient until recipient identity, recipient consent, and payment or payout setup have been verified. If payout verification is not completed within the required time window, TLWL may mark the campaign for refund review.

Campaign Use of Funds

Funds should be used according to the stated purpose on the campaign page. If circumstances change, TLWL may pause, revise, refund, or redirect funds according to the disclosed campaign terms and applicable law.

Reports and Fraud Prevention

Reports do not automatically remove a campaign. TLWL may review reports from visitors and may pause contributions, clear the campaign, remove the campaign, close the campaign, or mark the campaign for refund review if information appears false, misleading, fraudulent, harmful, or otherwise concerning.

Tax Notice

Contributions and TLWL support payments are not tax-deductible charitable donations unless specifically stated otherwise in writing.

Important: This starter language is not legal advice. Have a qualified attorney review before accepting public contributions.
