Refund Policy

This Refund Policy explains when refunds may or may not be available.

General Rule

Campaign contributions and TLWL support payments are generally final unless TLWL determines that a refund is appropriate, required by law, connected to a canceled or fraudulent campaign, or connected to a campaign marked for refund review.

Processing Fees

Payment processor fees may not be refundable depending on the payment provider.

Reported or Flagged Campaigns

If a campaign is reported, paused, removed, closed, or marked for refund review, TLWL may review contribution records and determine whether refunds or other actions are appropriate.

Payout Verification Issues

If recipient/payment verification is not completed within the required time window, TLWL may pause payouts, pause contributions, close the campaign, request additional information, or mark the campaign for refund review.

Refund Requests

Users should contact TLWL with the contribution date, amount, email used, and reason for the refund request.

Important: This starter language is not legal advice. Have a qualified attorney review before accepting public contributions.
