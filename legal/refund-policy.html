Refund Policy

This Refund Policy explains when refunds may or may not be available.

General Rule

Campaign contributions and TLWL support payments are generally final unless TLWL determines that a refund is appropriate, required by law, or connected to a canceled or fraudulent campaign.

Processing Fees

Payment processor fees may not be refundable depending on the payment provider.

Campaign Changes

If a campaign is canceled or cannot continue, TLWL may issue refunds, redirect funds to a similar purpose with notice, or take another reasonable action based on the campaign terms.

Refund Requests

Users should contact TLWL with the contribution date, amount, email used, and reason for the refund request.

Important: This starter language is not legal advice. Have a qualified attorney review before accepting public contributions.
