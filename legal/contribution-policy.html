Contribution Policy

This policy explains how campaign contributions and TLWL support payments may work on the platform.

Campaign Contributions

Campaign contributions may support specific campaigns, recipients, initiatives, or platform-approved purposes described on each campaign page.

Donate to TLWL

TLWL support payments help keep The Life We Live going by supporting platform work, storytelling, video content, outreach, website maintenance, and operations.

Tax Notice

Contributions and TLWL support payments are not tax-deductible charitable donations unless specifically stated otherwise in writing.

Fees

Payment processing fees, platform fees, or administrative costs may apply and should be disclosed clearly before accepting funds.

Important: This starter language is not legal advice. Have a qualified attorney review before accepting public contributions.
