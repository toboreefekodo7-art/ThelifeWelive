Contribution Policy

This policy explains how campaign contributions and TLWL support payments may work on the platform.

Campaign Contributions

Campaign contributions may support specific public stories, recipients, initiatives, or platform-listed purposes described on each campaign page. Contributions may open when required campaign information is complete and basic platform checks pass.

Contribution Pauses

TLWL may pause or disable contributions if a campaign receives serious reports, multiple reports, missing information, safety concerns, privacy concerns, fraud concerns, or other issues that require review.

Payout Verification

Campaign contributions should not be released to a recipient until recipient and payment setup verification are complete. A campaign may be public or accepting contributions while payout verification is still pending.

Donate to TLWL

TLWL support payments help keep The Life We Live going by supporting platform work, storytelling, video content, outreach, website maintenance, and operations. TLWL support payments are separate from campaign contributions.

Tax Notice

Contributions and TLWL support payments are not tax-deductible charitable donations unless specifically stated otherwise in writing.

Fees

Payment processing fees, platform fees, or administrative costs may apply and should be disclosed clearly before accepting funds.

Important: This starter language is not legal advice. Have a qualified attorney review before accepting public contributions.
