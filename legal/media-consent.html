Story Submission & Media Consent

Before TLWL publishes a person’s story, photo, video, interview, or likeness, TLWL should obtain clear consent.

Consent to Publish

Featured participants should agree that TLWL may record, edit, publish, and share their story across the website, social media, promotional materials, and campaign pages.

Truthful Submissions

Participants should confirm that the information they provide is accurate to the best of their knowledge.

Removal Requests

Participants may request content removal, but TLWL may not be able to remove third-party reposts, shares, screenshots, or archived versions outside TLWL’s control.

Separate Agreements

Any payment, ownership, or special arrangement should be handled in a separate written agreement.

Important: This starter language is not legal advice. Have a qualified attorney review before accepting public contributions.
